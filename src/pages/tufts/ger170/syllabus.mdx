## Goals of This Class

- Explain how fairy tales changed over time.
- Be able to use historical context to critical examine modern interpretations/retellings of fairy tales.

## Grade

- 20%: 3-page paper
  - Demonstrate your knowledge of how fairy tales changed from antiquity to the time of the Brothers Grimm.
- 40%: 6-8 page final paper
  - Analyze a modern adaptation of a Grimm fairy tale.
- 15%: writing your own short fairy tale (+ critical justification and interpretation)
- 25%: participation
  - Print and annotate readings for homework, then bring to class (no electronics).
  - Recommended to prepare 1-2 critical questions.

_Essays: Microsoft Word, 12pt Times New Roman_
