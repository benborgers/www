- **Stereotypes**: a generalized or simplistic description of a group. 
	- Children have far less experience with people so they’re more susceptible to stereotypes. 
	- Children learn through stereotyping: it simplifies and categorizes
	- Children’s TV often leans on stereotypes as a shortcut, because episodes are short and you have to get characters’ personalities across
