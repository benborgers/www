## Assignments

- Learning Artifacts, weekly (short reflections)
  - Due following Monday
  - ![](https://share.cleanshot.com/Fl8ZUQ/download)
- Group and individual homework, weekly
- 2 take-home exams
- Final group project
- Museum reflection (“for students who want to receive an A”)

Assignments are graded “Accept”, “Revise”, or “Missing”.

- Revised assignments must be re-submitted within 7 days of instructor feedback.
