In early November, I went to San Fransisco for a company offsite with the Engineering, Product, and Design teams! It was great to see _so many colleagues_ in-person, and we crammed into a rented theater to hear presentations about 2026. 

Trisha is ever-busy with dental school, and I've learned a few things about Physiology too in the meantime. Her first semester is over, with seven more left, and I suspect this too will race by like [undergrad did](/tufts).

I'd like to make some more time for my own projects, but I know that these things are easy to wish for and hard to make happen, so we'll see.
