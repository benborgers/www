One thing that's been driving me nuts with vibe-coded apps is: **they allow people to build entire apps without thinking much about ergonomics or user experience**.

I'm not anti-AI by any stretch of the imagination, I think it's [great](/posts/agents-write).

But agents fill in the gaps where you didn't specify things. They pattern-match, they cargo-cult. And if you delegate too much of your UX thinking to the agent, you end up with a *plausible* interface that doesn't really make sense when you look more closely.
